About

I’m NOX — an animator working in both 2D and 3D animation. Through different forms of movement, I express stories and emotions in motion.

I studied animation at Animation High School and continued my studies in animation at ChungKang University of Cultural Industries. Driven by a desire to create my own works, I explored every part of the production process, becoming a versatile generalist artist.
